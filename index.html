About

I am a trade economist interested in globalisation, firm behaviour, innovation, and technological change. My research examines how firms and industries respond to changes in international integration. In particular, I am interested in how those changes shape firms’ decisions about innovation and technological upgrading. To study these questions, I combine empirical analysis with theoretical modelling.
